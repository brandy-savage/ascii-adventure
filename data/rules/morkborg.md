# MörK Borg — Rules Reference

> "A doom metal album of a game." A pitch-black fantasy RPG of violence, misery, and the end of the world.

## Core Mechanic
Roll **d20 + ability modifier**, meet or beat the **DR (Difficulty Rating)**.
- Easy: DR 10 · Normal: DR 12 · Hard: DR 14 · Extreme: DR 16

Attack rolls use the same system — beat the target's armor DR.

**Strength (STR)** — melee attacks, feats of muscle
**Agility (AGI)** — ranged attacks, defense, stealth, initiative
**Presence (PRE)** — morale, powers, persuasion
**Toughness (TOU)** — endurance, resisting damage

## HP
**HP = Toughness modifier + d8** (min 1). Can never exceed starting max.

## Omens
Roll a **d2 (coin flip)** — that many Omens per session.
Spend an Omen to reroll any die. Once gone, they're gone.

## Combat
- Initiative: highest AGI goes first (ties: both roll d4)
- Attack: d20 + STR or AGI vs target's armor DR
- Damage: weapon die (see equipment)
- Defense: light armor DR12 / med DR14 / heavy DR16
- No armor: DR 10

## Death
At 0 HP, roll on the **Broken** table (d6):
1. Dead. Make a new character.
2–4. Critically injured — take a permanent scar/disability, survive.
5–6. Down but not out — unconscious, need help.

## Powers (if applicable)
Roll **PRE + d20 vs DR12** to use a power. On failure, roll on **Misuse of Powers** table.

## Equipment
Starting silver: roll **d6 × 10**.
Roll on random gear tables or pick from equipment list.
Encumbrance: carry STR modifier + 8 items (min 4).

## The World Ends
At the start of each session, roll **d6** on the **Miseries of the Dying World** table.
On a 1, something catastrophic happens to the world.
