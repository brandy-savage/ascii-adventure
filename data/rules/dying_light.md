# DYING LIGHT // ENDLESS NIGHT — Rules Reference

*MörK Borg × CY_BORG hybrid. 5 sessions. High lethality.*

---

## Core Mechanic
Roll **d20 + ability modifier** vs **DR 12** (default).
Enemies usually don't roll — you defend against their actions.

## The Five Abilities
Roll **3d6** per ability at character creation.

| Roll | Modifier |
|------|----------|
| 1–4  | −3       |
| 5–6  | −2       |
| 7–8  | −1       |
| 9–12 |  0       |
| 13–14| +1       |
| 15–16| +2       |
| 17–18| +3       |

- **STR** — Melee, forcing, lifting
- **AGI** — Speed, stealth, dodging
- **PRE** — Social, morale, fear resistance
- **TOU** — Damage resistance, environmental exposure
- **KNOWLEDGE** — Hacking, medicine, artifact analysis, navigation, lore
  *(replaces SYNTH/Intelligence for all Knowledge-keyed abilities)*

**HP:** TOU modifier + d8 (minimum 1)
**Starting chips:** d6 × 10
**Omens:** d2 (reroll a die once per omen, then it's spent)

---

## THE DOOM CLOCK
Each session at dawn the GM rolls. On a 1, one sun segment is lost.
6 segments total. When the last goes out — the lights go out permanently.

| Sessions | Die  |
|----------|------|
| 1–2      | d6   |
| 3–4      | d4   |
| 5        | d2   |

## LIGHT IS SURVIVAL

| Source            | Duration | Notes                               |
|-------------------|----------|-------------------------------------|
| Chemical Flare    | 10 min   | 30ft bright. Single use.            |
| Torch             | 1 hour   | Extinguished by wind/water. Attracts Gloamers. |
| Battery Lamp      | 3 hours  | Electric, steady. Attracts different things. |
| Neon Canister     | 6 hours  | Black market, 200 chips. Daylight in 10ft. |
| The Artifact      | ∞ ?      | Cannot be hidden. Cannot be extinguished. |

**In darkness:** All PRE and AGI tests are DR+4.
Each round in total darkness: test PRE DR12 or lose your action to panic.

**The Attention Rule:** Brighter light = wider detection radius.
Torches attract sight-hunters within ~1 mile. Battery lamps attract other hunters.
The artifact cannot be concealed from anything within range.

---

## AIR IS SURVIVAL

Outside Lux-9, the atmosphere kills.
- **Unprotected:** symptoms at 24h, critical at 48h
- **Basic respirator:** several days
- **Full filter suit:** indefinite — until seals crack, filters clog

---

## THE WORLD

**Lux-9** — Last megacity on Earth. 300 million people. Neon never turns off.
**The Black Earth** — Toxic smog. Permanent night. Things that evolved to hunt in it.
**The Luminary Conclave** — Megacorps + techno-priests + death cults. They hoard light.
