# Dying Light — Custom Rules

> A custom campaign system built on MörK Borg / CY_BORG foundations.
> Full rules: https://github.com/ahhh/Dying-Light/blob/main/for_players/how_to_play.md

## System Base
Dying Light uses the **CY_BORG** core mechanic (d20 + ability vs DR) with
custom classes, factions, and setting lore layered on top.

All MörK Borg and CY_BORG rules apply unless explicitly overridden here.

## Setting
A post-collapse city where corporate enclaves and anarchist communes fight
for scraps of the old world. Magic and tech coexist uncomfortably.
The Dying Light is what they call the last signal — a broadcast nobody
can identify, playing on repeat from somewhere beneath the city.
