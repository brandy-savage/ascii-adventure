# CY_BORG — Rules Reference

> MörK Borg in a neon-lit cyberpunk hell. Same bleak misery, now with hacking and cyberware.

## Core Mechanic — same as MörK Borg
Roll **d20 + ability modifier**, meet or beat the **DR**.
- Easy: DR 10 · Normal: DR 12 · Hard: DR 14 · Extreme: DR 16

## Abilities (same 3d6 table as MörK Borg)
**Strength (STR)** — muscle, melee
**Agility (AGI)** — speed, ranged, stealth
**Presence (PRE)** — social, nano-powers, morale
**Toughness (TOU)** — endurance, injury resistance
**SYNTH** — hacking, tech interaction, cyber resistance *(CY_BORG exclusive)*

## HP & Glitch
**HP = TOU modifier + d8** (min 1)
**Glitch = d4** per session (like Omens) — spend to reroll, or for nano-powers

## Classes (Archetypes)
- **Nano-Mystic** — street shaman, nano-powers, reality glitches
- **Renegade** — ex-corps soldier, weapons, tactics
- **Abandoned** — discarded android, tough, emotion-learning
- **Dock Rat** — street survivor, scavenging, connections
- **Data Witch** — pure hacker, ICE-breaking, ghost-in-the-net
- **Chrome Avenger** — bounty hunter, cyberware-heavy
- **Flesh Merchant** — black-market biotech dealer
- **Punk** — chaos agent, demolitions, spectacle

## Cyberware
Each piece of cyberware grants a bonus but adds to your **Cyberpsychosis track**.
Roll TOU when installing new ware: fail = gain a Glitch (negative).

Common cyberware:
- **Subdermal Armor** — +1 armor DR
- **Chrome Arm** — +2 STR for melee, weapon built-in
- **Neural Jack** — +2 SYNTH, direct machine interface
- **Optic Implant** — +2 AGI for ranged, thermal/night vision
- **Voice Modulator** — +2 PRE for social manipulation
- **Reflex Booster** — always act first in initiative

## Hacking
Roll **d20 + SYNTH vs system DR** (usually DR 12–16).
- Success: gain access, plant virus, steal data, crash system
- Failure: traced — NPC corp sends response team in d6 turns
- Critical fail (1): Black ICE — take d6 damage directly to HP

## Credits
Starting credits: **d6 × 100**.
Replace MörK Borg silver. Everything costs more in the neon city.

## The City Dies
Same end-times framework as MörK Borg. Roll d6 each session for **Apocalypse Track**:
- 1: Corporate crackdown — random area under lockdown
- 2: Blackout — grid goes dark, tech fails on DR 1–3
- 3: Riots — streets become combat zones
- 4: Plague — disease spreading through the slums
- 5: Drone swarm — automated lethal force deployed
- 6: Nuke it — something very bad is about to happen
