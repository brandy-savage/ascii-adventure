# Campaign: Shadows Over Neverwinter

## Setting
The year is 1492 DR. The city of Neverwinter rebuilds after decades of catastrophe, but dark forces stir beneath its cobblestone streets. A cult called the Ashen Hand has been kidnapping citizens, and the Lords' Alliance suspects a lich named Mordavian the Pale is pulling the strings from the ruins of Helm's Hold.

## Tone
Gritty, high-stakes, noir-tinged fantasy. Think dark taverns, political intrigue, and ancient horrors. Moments of levity exist but death is real.

## The Party
The players are freelance adventurers hired by Soman Galt, the city's harbormaster, to investigate disappearances in the Docks District.

## Key Locations
- **The Driftwood Tavern** — Party's home base. Run by a half-orc named Greta. Cheap ale, good information.
- **Helm's Hold** — Ruined monastery east of the city. Ashen Hand's suspected base.
- **The Underdock** — Flooded tunnels beneath the harbor. Strange lights seen at night.
- **Tower of Arcturia** — Abandoned wizard's tower. Magical anomalies reported.

## Key NPCs
- **Soman Galt** — Harbormaster, party's employer. Nervous, hides something.
- **Mordavian the Pale** — Ancient lich. Calculating, patient, obsessed with undeath research.
- **Sister Garaele** — Harper agent posing as a cleric of Tymora. Ally.
- **Vorgax** — Goblin information broker in the Docks. Greedy but reliable.

## Current Plot Hook
Three dockworkers vanished from Pier 7 last night. Witnesses report seeing hooded figures and smelling sulfur. A child says she saw "a man with no face."

## House Rules
- Death saves are private (DM rolls in secret)
- Inspiration can be earned through good roleplay
- No evil PCs
