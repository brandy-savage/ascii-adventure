# Campaign: The Generic Realm

## Setting
A classic high-fantasy world of swords, sorcery, and adventure. Kingdoms rise and fall, dragons sleep in mountain caves, and brave heroes venture into dungeons for glory and gold.

## Tone
Classic D&D. Fun, adventurous, anything goes. Mix of serious moments and comedy. Player agency matters.

## Key Locations
- **The Crossroads Inn** — Where all adventures begin.
- **The Dungeon** — A mysterious dungeon of unknown depth below the old keep.
- **The Dark Forest** — Wolves, goblins, and stranger things lurk within.
- **The Capital City** — Political intrigue, guilds, and endless side quests.

## House Rules
Standard 5e rules apply. Rule of cool encouraged.
